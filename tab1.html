Gl*tch in the Ro*tine


These two videos were a project that I worked on with a friend of mine also in the DMA major. The challenge was to make an installation and we chose to do an online exhibit to display the video we worked on. Although the website no longer works, I saved these two videos which was the main part of the site. It is a vr video meant to be worn through either a vr or google cardboard headset. It similates someone watching the different lives of students and what they need to do to keep up their appearance on social media.
