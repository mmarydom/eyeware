Are You Aware?

Hello! My name is Mary Dominguez and welcome to my site. In here, we explore the madness of intervening with ones life by peeking inside the lens of a webcam. Are you aware that you are being watched at this moment? Well look around you. you are currently holding a portable security camera, being watched and judged by millions of "followers." My website features videos and screenshots of the work I have done throughout the semester. All of which hold the same theme - the breeching of our privacy and how it can deteriorate our mental state.



Remember... Big Brother is Always Watching :)
